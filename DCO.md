# Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO)

By contributing, you certify that:

1. The contribution was created in whole or in part by you and you have the right to submit it under the open source license indicated in the file; or
2. The contribution is based on previous work that is covered under an appropriate open source license; or
3. The contribution was provided directly to you by someone who certified (1) or (2) and you have not modified it; and
4. You understand and agree that this project and the contribution are public and that a record of the contribution (including your sign-off) is maintained indefinitely.

Please sign off commits with:

```
Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>
```

Use `git commit -s` to add the sign-off automatically.
